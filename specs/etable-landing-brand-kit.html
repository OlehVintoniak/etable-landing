01 — Brand Foundation

Who We Are

Коріння (Korinna) means roots in Ukrainian. Every table we make begins with a single slab of native hardwood — oak from Polissia, walnut from Poltava, ash from the Carpathians — chosen for its grain, its age, its story.

Epoxy is not the product. It is the vessel. A way to preserve the raw beauty of wood — its cracks, voids, and imperfections — so that a piece of nature can live in your home forever.

We make furniture for people who want to own something real.

01

Wood First

Every piece begins with the slab. The wood defines the table — epoxy serves it, never overshadows it.

02

Ukrainian by Nature

We source from local forests and sawmills. Every table carries a piece of Ukrainian landscape.

03

Made to Last Generations

We don't make trends. We make heirlooms. Each piece is built to be passed down, not replaced.

"I make heirloom furniture from Ukrainian hardwood — tables that carry the story of the forest they came from, built to last generations."

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Speak

✓ We Sound Like

Warm, unhurried, craftsman-proud
We tell the story of the wood — its age, origin, grain
We use specific, sensory language ("the crack runs 40cm along the grain")
We are proud of being Ukrainian without being loud about it
We speak of time — decades, generations, centuries
We let silence (white space) breathe between ideas

✗ We Never Sound Like

Salesy or pushy ("LIMITED TIME OFFER!")
Generic ("high-quality handmade furniture")
Trend-chasing ("the hottest epoxy style of 2026")
Over-humble ("just a small workshop")
Corporate ("our products feature premium materials")
Rushed — we never undersell the craft

07 — Product Naming

How to Name Your Tables

Every table gets a name rooted in its identity — the wood species, its origin, or a characteristic. This turns a product into a story.

Polissia

Stepa

Formula: [Ukrainian Region or Natural Feature] + wood species + epoxy color in product sheet. Ref code: KOR-[SPECIES]-[NUMBER] (e.g. KOR-OAK-001)
